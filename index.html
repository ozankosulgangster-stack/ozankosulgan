About Me

From telecom delivery discipline to software-centric AI and data execution.

My journey started in telecom, where successful delivery depends on reliability, operational readiness, vendor coordination, customer impact, and disciplined stakeholder communication. That foundation shaped how I think about complex work: the plan has to be clear, the risks have to be visible, and every team needs to understand how their work connects to the outcome.

As delivery has become more software-centric, my focus has evolved toward AI project management, data project delivery, agile execution, PMO governance, and adoption-led change. I work at the intersection of business goals and technical teams, translating AI and data ideas into structured, deliverable business outcomes.
